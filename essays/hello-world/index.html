Hello, World

Welcome! This my personal website and its initial setup right now: GitHub as host, Hugo as the blogging engine, and this theme heavily modified . Although there is plenty to fix and finish, a start’s a start. Hugo definitely presents a far steeper learning curve than Jekyll, and its benefits are not apparent yet. But, giving any tech a proper runthrough is necessary, and I consider a proper runthrough as getting to a “production” MVP.

In the coming weeks, I will be posting quite frequently, but this pace should not be considered a norm. Rather, it’s a way for me to get through my large backlog of writing more quickly. I enjoy longer writing with better depth than short bursts of quick thought; so, I perfer infrequent updates. That runs contrary to literally all of the web, but being a personal website I feel my personal preferences should take priority.
